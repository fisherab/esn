Bringing nature to you

Welcome to Eye Spy Nature.

We offer a comprehensive range of nestboxes and cameras for watching birds in their home from the comfort of your home.

We provide the option to connect the nestbox to your TV using wire or wireless transmission allowing the nestbox to be placed up to 1km away.
